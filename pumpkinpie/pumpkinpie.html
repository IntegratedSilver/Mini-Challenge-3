Pumpkin Pie

Pumpkin pie is a beloved symbol of autumn and holiday gatherings in many households. Perfect for those autum nights and those wonderful days. Here, I'll tell give you a step by step guide on how to make them!





Ingredients

  • Cooking spray
  • 1 tbsp. all-purpose flour, plus more for rolling
  • 1 batch basic pie crust, chilled
  • 3 large eggs, beaten to blend
  • 1 (15-oz.) can pumpkin puree
  • 3/4 cups of packed brown sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups of heavy cream
  • 2 tsp. pumpkin pie spice
  • 1 tsp. pure vanilla extract
  • 1/4 tsp. kosher salt
  • Whipped cream, for serving (optional)
    •

    Directions

    1. Preheat oven to 375°
    2. Place a baking sheet in oven
    3. Lightly grease a 9"-x-1.5" pie dish with cooking spray
    4. On a lightly floured surface, roll dough to a 12" circle
    5. Drape over pie dish and gently press to fit, making sure there are no air bubbles
    6. Trim overhang to 1" and tuck under itself
    7. Crimp edges as desired then chill in refrigerator until cold for at least 30 minutes or up to 3 hours
    8. Line dough with foil or parchment and fill with dried beans or pie weights
    9. Bake crust until top and sides are dry and set, about 15 to 18 minutes, then remove foil and weights
    10. Continue to bake until the bottom is dry to the touch for about 5 more minutes
    11. Let cool
    12. Reduce oven to 350º
    13. In a large bowl, whisk eggs, pumpkin, brown sugar, cream, pumpkin pie spice, vanilla, salt, and flour until smooth
    14. Pour pumpkin mixture into crust
    15. Carefully place pie dish on heated sheet
    16. Bake pie until filling is slightly jiggly in the center and crust is golden brown, about 45 to 50 minutes
    17. Turn off oven, prop door open, and let pie cool 30 minutes
    18. Transfer pan to a wire rack and let cool completely
    19. Serve with whipped cream (if using)
      20.

    Serve And Enjoy!
