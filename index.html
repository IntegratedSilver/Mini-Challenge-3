Stuffed Bell Peppers

Stuffed Bell Peppers can be customized with a variety of ingredients, making them a popular and adaptable meal in many cuisines around the world. Here, I'll tell give you a step by step guide on how to make them!





Ingredients

  • 1/2 cups of uncooked white or brown rice
  • 2 tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil, plus more for drizzling
  • 1 medium yellow onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp. tomato paste
  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 1 (14.5oz.) can diced tomatoes
  • 1 1/2 tsp. dried oregano
  • Kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 6 bell peppers, tops and cores removed
  • 1 cup of shredded Monterey jack
  • Chopped fresh parsley, for serving

Directions

  1. In a large skillet over medium heat, heat oil
  2. Cook onions, stirring occasionally until softened for about 7 minutes
  3. Stir in garlic and tomato paste and cook until fragrant for about 1 more minute
  4. Add ground beef and cook, breaking up meat with a spoon until beef is no longer pink. Do this for about 6 minutes
  5. Drain excess fat
  6. Stir in rice and diced tomatoes and season with oregano, salt, and pepper
  7. Let simmer while stirring occasionally, until liquid has reduced slightly for about 5 minutes
  8. Arrange peppers cut side up in a 13"x9" baking dish and drizzle with oil
  9. Spoon beef mixture into each pepper
  10. Top peppers with cheese, then cover baking dish with foil
  11. Bake peppers until tender, about 35 minutes
  12. Uncover and continue to bake until cheese is bubbly for about 10 more minutes
  13. Top with parsley before serving

Serve And Enjoy!
